Münster

Münster might be most famous for the city of the westphalian peace treaty, with which on 24. Oktober 1848 the 30-year war ended. More than 350 years later münster has quite a lot more to offer.
Bars, museums and many beautiful places wait for you to be discovered. And then there are also the more than 100 smaller and larger castles in the country side.
