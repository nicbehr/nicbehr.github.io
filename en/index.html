Arrival

To Münster

Via Airplane

Airport Münster-Osnabrück (FMO), then via Taxi or Bus (S50), which brings you directly to the main train station (ca. 30 minutes)
Airport Düsseldorf (DUS), then via train (direct or 1 transfer) in ca. 1,5 hours
Airport Amsterdam-Schipol (AMS), then take the train via Enschede (3,5 – 4 hours)

Via Train

Münster is well connected via the central train station. From there you can reach the registry of Münster in about 15 Minutes (by foot) and the party venue in ca. 20 Minutes (by foot). Many hotels and B&Bs can be found nearby.

Via car

There is only limited parking close to the registry of Münster. So please use parking lots in the vicinity like the one close to the Hörsterplatz (Korduanenstraße 9), the Theater (Tibusstraße 2) or at Alter Steinweg 21. At the esCape there are plenty of parking spots available.

On the Day of the Wedding

In Münster, the best and most comfortable mean of transportation is definitely the bike. You can rent one at the Radstation“ which is located in front of the main train station (exit towards city center ( https://www.radstation.de/mieten ).

You can also use an E-Scooter, e.g. the turquoise/mint colored ones from TIER that you can use via the TIER-app.

You can call a taxi via the central hotline 0049-251-6011.

Münster

The history buffs amongst you might know Münster as the city of the Westphalian Peace Treaty, which ended the Thirty Year War on the 24th of October 1648. Other than that, Münster is a town of bikes and students with a wonderful farmers market near the Dome. We love not only the historic city center but also the colorful and lively quarters like the Hansa- and the Hafenviertel (where we live), the Südviertel close to the Hammerstraße, the Erphoviertel at the Warendorfer Straße, das Kreuzviertel and many many more. Last but not least, you can visit more than 100 big and small castles in the countryside with a (road-)bike.
